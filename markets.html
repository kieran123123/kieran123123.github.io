Our markets

Emerging markets represent an exceptional opportunity for the pharmaceutical industry and while succeeding in emerging markets has been a challenging undertaking for companies those that adapt their business models to serve these emerging markets with strategic partners can thrive.

Africa's share alone of the global population is projected to grow to 25% by 2050 and the tropical climate of Africa makes the continent one of the largest reservoirs of infectious diseases, and when combined with the paradigm shift in the adoption of a Western lifestyle there has been a significant increase in non-communicable diseases, driving the demand for chronic prescription medication.
