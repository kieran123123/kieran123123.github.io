Our technology portfolio speaks for itself

MEDPORT has partnered with SECHO® Technologies, whose development of a hybrid "distributed ledger technology" based on block-chain offers an important asset in flow-management, identification, authentication and the traceability of products throughout the logistics and patient supply chain.

SECHO® provides an independently verifiable stage completion mechanism for the set-up of contractual handshakes, goods release and payments or guarantees without third-parties. This proprietary platform also provides data driven theft patterns to design prevention methods with the help of Machine Learning (ML).
